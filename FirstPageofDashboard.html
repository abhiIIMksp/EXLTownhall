What is CDRM?

CDRM (Customer Data Risk Management) represents an advanced, multi-layered framework designed to systematically identify, classify, and safeguard sensitive customer information within cloud-based data ecosystems.

The system goes beyond basic keyword matching, employing semantic analysis to understand context, transformer-based embeddings for nuanced term relationships, and regex validation for precise pattern detection.

Technical Highlights

Comprehensive Dictionaries

Our domain-specific dictionaries contain exhaustive mappings of column categories and their variants. For example, our vehicle dictionary includes terms like Vin which maps to multiple forms like Vehicle_Ident_number, Vin_nbr, and Vin_id.

Advanced AI Matching

We use transformer embeddings and cosine similarity to match column names with dictionary terms, achieving industry-leading accuracy in classification.

Two-Layer Validation

Every classification undergoes both name-based pattern matching and value-based regex validation to ensure maximum accuracy and reliability.
